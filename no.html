About

Every 98 seconds, an American is sexually assaulted. El Sol is a website intended to aid victims and people who support them. It can be difficult trying to start the healing process, we want to show that you are not alone. On our site you will find pictures, advice, and hotlines on this topic.

#MeToo & #TimesUp

#MeToo and #TimesUp are movements against sexual harassment and assault. They are hashtags used on social media in an attempt to demonstrate the widespread prevalence of sexual assault and harassment, especially in the workplace.
